SLIDE 2 — THE PROBLEM
===================================================

[LEFT ALIGNED HEADING | RIGHT SIDE: LARGE EMOJI ILLUSTRATION]

HEADING: The Problem With Life Decisions Today

Every year, millions of students face questions like:
• "Should I take this job offer or do my Masters?"
• "Is my startup idea worth quitting college for?"
• "Which university should I apply to based on my JEE score?"

Existing tools fail them:
❌ Generic Google searches return one-size-fits-all advice
❌ AI chatbots give confident but fabricated information
❌ No tool considers their REAL situation — savings, risk tolerance, score cutoffs, local job market

Result: Bad decisions made on bad information.

===================================================
SLIDE 3 — THE SOLUTION
===================================================

[CENTERED, BOLD]

HEADING: Introducing LifeLens

LifeLens is an AI-powered conversational decision engine that:

✅ Collects your real context (scores, savings, location, goals)
✅ Runs a structured multi-turn AI consultation
✅ Produces a personalized Decision Dossier with tradeoffs, alternatives, and action plans
✅ Connects you to real human counselors if emotional distress is detected

"Not just an answer — a structured reasoning journey."

===================================================

[THREE COLUMN CARD LAYOUT]

===================================================
SLIDE 5 — HOW IT WORKS (ARCHITECTURE)
===================================================

[CENTER — CLEAN FLOW DIAGRAM]

HEADING: Under The Hood

[ User Fills Context Form ]
        ↓
[ Express Backend Validates + Builds System Prompt ]
        ↓
[ Sarvam AI / OpenAI / Groq LLM Responds ]
        ↓
[ Structured JSON Parsed & Rendered as Dossier ]
        ↓
[ If Distress Detected → Counselor Card Shown ]
        ↓
[ Firebase Auth → Session Verified ]

Tech Stack:
• Frontend: React + Vite + Vanilla CSS
• Backend: Node.js + Express
• AI Models: OpenAI GPT-4o-mini, Groq Llama-3.3-70B, Sarvam AI (Indic LLM)
• Auth: Firebase Authentication (Google + Email)
• Deployment: Vercel + Render (Production) / Base44 (Live)

===================================================
SLIDE 6 — SARVAM AI INTEGRATION
===================================================

[LEFT: INDIA FLAG ICON | RIGHT: TEXT]

HEADING: Built for India, Powered by Sarvam AI

LifeLens integrates Sarvam AI as its Indic-language layer.
This means the app can understand and respond in:
Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Bengali, Marathi

Use Cases:
• Students who describe their situation in Hindi get responses in Hindi
• Resume parsing handles mixed Indic-English content accurately
• Decision dossiers are translated dynamically to user's native language

Endpoint: https://api.sarvam.ai/v1/chat/completions
Model: sarvam-2b-v0.5
