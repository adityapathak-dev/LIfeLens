# Video Script: LifeLens — 2-Minute Pitch & Demo

---

## Part 1: The Hook (0:00 - 0:20)
*   **Visual on Screen:** Close-up of the speaker or a high-level screen recording showing the LifeLens homepage. The header "Make your next move with clarity" is visible.
*   **Audio (Narrator):**
    > "Choosing a graduate school, accepting a job offer, or deciding to launch a startup. These are some of the most high-stakes, stressful decisions we make in our lives. 
    > But if you search the web or ask a typical chatbot, you’re usually hit with generic lists of pros and cons that ignore your personal context, leaving you more anxious than before. 
    > That’s why we built LifeLens."

---

## Part 2: The Core Concept & Intake (0:20 - 0:50)
*   **Visual on Screen:** Screen recording transitions to clicking "Startup Advisor," then navigating the intake form. Show inputting location, targeted sector, and runway. Highlight the progress bar filling up dynamically.
*   **Audio (Narrator):**
    > "LifeLens is a conversational decision advisor that doesn't make choices for you. Instead, it makes the structure of your decision visible. 
    > It starts with a context-sensitive intake form. As I select my path, the UI adjusts dynamically. It tracks my parameters, validates test scores, maps out my financial runway, and measures my completion progress.
    > Notice how inputs slide in smoothly and text areas expand automatically to keep the interface clean."

---

## Part 3: Guided Chat & Dossier (0:50 - 1:20)
*   **Visual on Screen:** Show the transition to the chat interface. Record typing a message and receiving a reply. Highlight the AI asking exactly one clear question. After a couple of turns, show the "Decision Dossier" table rendering inline.
*   **Audio (Narrator):**
    > "Once inside the chat, LifeLens acts as an objective advisor. It queries my assumptions one step at a time to examine factors like cost of living, reputation, and visa rules.
    > At the end of the consultation, it doesn't give a recommendation. Instead, it generates a structured Decision Dossier. This breaks down my short-term and long-term tradeoffs, reveals hidden assumptions, and attaches a confidence rating based on how complete my data was."

---

## Part 4: Special Tools & Safety (1:20 - 1:45)
*   **Visual on Screen:** Show clicking back to select "Job Offer" to run a Resume ATS check, or click "Startup Evaluator" to show the Idea Meter scoring tool. Then show a counselor fallback card pop up in chat.
*   **Audio (Narrator):**
    > "LifeLens also packs tools like an AI Resume ATS checker for job seekers and a brutal Idea Meter for startup founders.
    > Crucially, we designed this with Responsible AI in mind. AI is not a therapist or a professional counselor. If the system detects distress or high confusion, it instantly bypasses the LLM to display a localized, real-world counselor hotline based on the user's location."

---

## Part 5: Outro & Call to Action (1:45 - 2:00)
*   **Visual on Screen:** Transition back to the speaker or show the final "Thank You" screen with the GitHub repository link and Vercel URL.
*   **Audio (Narrator):**
    > "LifeLens is fully deployed on Vercel as a unified monorepo. It structures complex choices so that the final human decision remains fully informed. 
    > Try it out today, check out our repository on GitHub, and take control of your next big move. Thank you."
